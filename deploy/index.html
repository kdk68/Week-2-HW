The thirst for a new way of telling a story

Starting with a camera as a brush, a lens as a dab of paint

"Photography is the story I fail to put into words" -Destin Sparks

David Kim is a design student with an ever-growing passion for photography. Starting with the camera of an iPod Touch 7 years ago, he would always try to find creative ways to share his experiences with others. Today, David Kim keeps developing his skill in photography, leaning more into product and portrait photography.
